Dr. Kwabena A. N. Sarpong

Director
X

Dr. Kwabena A. N. Sarpong


He is the founder and director of Sarpong Research Consult. He holds a PhD in Biochemistry and Molecular Biophysics at the Prestigious Washington University School of Medicine in St Louis, MO, USA. He is a proud past student of St. Augustine's College, Cape Coast, Ghana, and has BSc in Biochemistry from the University of Ghana, Legon. He spent his last semester on a Commonwealth scholarship at Brock University in Canada. He then pursued a Master’s degree in Chemistry at the Missouri State University, USA where he specialized in bio-organic chemistry. Koby has worked as a consultant for the Biotechnology And Life Sciences Advising company in St. Louis, USA. He is also an entrepreneur and an investor in the areas of agribusiness, home tutoring and corporate branding. Koby is a passionate educator, having taught science at both the high school and college levels. He has in-depth knowledge of educational systems across the globe and has been involved in mentoring programs for junior and senior high school students, providing seminars, workshops and science demonstrations.
